Browser Extension Security Review Report

Date: [23/02/2026]
Browser: Google Chrome

Installed Extensions Observed:
1. Adobe Photoshop
2. McAfee WebAdvisor
3. Photoshop Online

Analysis:
- Adobe Photoshop is an official extension from Adobe and is used for legitimate image editing tasks.
- McAfee WebAdvisor provides security-related features such as warning against malicious websites.
- Photoshop Online is a third-party extension providing similar functionality to Adobe Photoshop.

Action Taken:
- Photoshop Online extension was removed due to redundancy and to minimize unnecessary permissions.

Security Impact:
- Reduced risk from third-party extensions
- Improved browser hygiene
- Lowered attack surface

Final Status:
Only essential and trusted browser extensions remain installed.
